Changing Grocery Shopping Forever

Overview:

Our invention, in phase 0 of development, is a grocery store navigation app that converts a list of recipes to a shopping list and then the shopping list into a route of the destination grocery store based on the store layout. This route will provide customers the shortest route to pick up their items, leading to an improved customer experience.

Additional Details:

You can think about this app as Google Maps for your grocery list. You could either directly select the items you want to purchase or upload a shopping list from a file or photo or import recipe ingredients needed. This is designed for people who don't want to spend an hour or two browsing for groceries. You know what you want and now you want to know exactly where to get it.
